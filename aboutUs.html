About Jackpot.com

Jackpot.com is a third-party app and website that facilitates the purchase of official state lottery tickets online.

Enjoy from Everywhere

Order your lottery ticket from your phone, tablet, or computer.

See your Ticket

Choose the lottery, pick your numbers, and see a scan of the tickets in your private account, confirming the purchase and proving your ownership.

All the Games you Love

All the lottery games offered in your State are available at your fingertips.

Secure and Safe

Our experienced team ensures your tickets are purchased, processed, and stored securely and that your personal details and account information are safeguarded.
