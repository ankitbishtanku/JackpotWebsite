Comparing your lottery numbers

This page is updated after each and every draw with the latest lottery results so you will also be able to compare your numbers - which can be found in your account - to the winning numbers.

You can also get lottery results alerts sent to you by email completely free of charge! To sign up for these alerts, go to the Results page, choose the lottery and select the ‘Get free results’ button. If you change your mind, you can simply unsubscribe and the results will stop being sent immediately.

Keep your eyes peeled with all the important results by bookmarking this page (Press CTRL-D).
